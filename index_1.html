About

I am a PhD Research Scholar at the Centre for Computational and Data Sciences, IIT Kharagpur, specializing in Information Retrieval, Large Language Models, and In-Context Learning. My research focuses on advancing AI and ML methodologies, particularly in the domains of few-shot learning for retrieval systems and generative AI applications.

Currently working with the AI4ICPS Technology Innovation Hub, I have gained recognition for developing innovative AI-based evaluation frameworks and contributing to the advancement of information retrieval systems. My work spans across medical information systems, educational evaluation, and crowd computing applications.

With a strong foundation in both theoretical research and practical applications, I have published in top-tier conferences including EMNLP 2024 and have accumulated over 331 citations for my research contributions. My academic journey is driven by the goal of making AI systems more efficient, interpretable, and accessible across various domains.
